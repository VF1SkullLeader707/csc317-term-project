Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about OrbitCart, ordering, and this course project.

Is OrbitCart a real store?

No. OrbitCart is a course project created for CSC 317 at San Francisco State University. All products, payments, and orders are simulated for educational purposes.

Do I need an account to shop?

You can browse products without an account. Creating an account allows you to save items, complete checkout, and view your purchase history.

How does checkout work?

Checkout is mocked. Orders are stored in the database and appear in your profile, but no real payments are processed.

Are payments real?

No. OrbitCart does not connect to any real payment system or collect financial information.

What technologies are used?

OrbitCart is built using Node.js, Express, SQLite, and vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
