Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about OrbitCart, ordering, and this course project.

Is OrbitCart a real store?

No. OrbitCart is a course project created for CSC 317 at San Francisco State University. All products, payments, and orders are simulated for educational purposes only.

Do I need an account to shop?

You can browse products without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save items, complete checkout, and view your purchase history in your user profile.

How does checkout work?

Checkout is a mocked process. When you place an order, the system records the purchase in the database and displays it in your profile‚Äôs purchase history. No real payment processing is performed.

Are payments real?

No. All payments are simulated. OrbitCart does not connect to any real payment gateway or collect real financial information.

What technologies are used?

OrbitCart is built using Node.js, Express, SQLite, and vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript. User authentication, product browsing, and cart logic are implemented server-side and client-side.
